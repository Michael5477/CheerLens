Frequently Asked Questions

How does CheerLens work?

CheerLens uses your device's camera and Google ML Kit Face Detection to analyze your facial expressions in real-time. All processing happens locally on your device—your data never leaves your phone.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! CheerLens works completely offline. The only time you need internet is for downloading the app and managing subscriptions through the App Store.

Is my data private?

Absolutely! All facial analysis is processed locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Your privacy is our top priority.

What's included in the free trial?

The free trial gives you full access to all Premium features for 3 days. After the trial, you can continue with Premium or use the free plan with 3 minutes of daily practice.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through the App Store:

  1. Open Settings on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Select CheerLens
  5. Choose to cancel or modify your subscription

You can also cancel directly from the app by going to Settings → Manage Subscription.

Can I use CheerLens on multiple devices?

Yes! Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID, so you can use CheerLens on any device signed in with the same Apple ID.

What devices are supported?

CheerLens requires iOS 16.6 or later and is optimized for iPhone and iPad. The app requires a front-facing camera for facial expression analysis.

I'm having technical issues. What should I do?

If you're experiencing technical problems:

  1. Make sure you've granted camera permissions to CheerLens
  2. Ensure you're using iOS 16.6 or later
  3. Try restarting the app
  4. If issues persist, contact us at michaellin5477@gmail.com with details about the issue

How do I provide feedback?

We love hearing from you! You can provide feedback:

  • Through the app: Settings → Support → Send Feedback

Tips for Best Results

  • Good Lighting: Ensure you're in a well-lit area so the camera can clearly see your face
  • Face the Camera: Position yourself so your face is clearly visible in the camera view
  • Practice Regularly: Consistent practice will help you improve your smile confidence
  • Be Natural: Try to smile naturally—forced smiles are less effective

App Features

CheerLens offers two practice modes:

  • Smile Training (Q&A Practice): Answer interview questions while maintaining your smile
  • Smile Monitoring: Practice speaking naturally while monitoring your smile throughout

Both modes provide real-time feedback and detailed analytics to help you improve.
