Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

Your Privacy is Our Priority

CheerLens is designed with privacy at its core. We believe your personal data should remain yours, and we've built our app to ensure maximum privacy and security.

Local Processing Only

All facial expression analysis in CheerLens is processed locally on your device using Google ML Kit Face Detection technology. This means:

  • Your facial data never leaves your device
  • No images or video are stored
  • No data is transmitted to any server
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality

What Data We Don't Collect

CheerLens does NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • Facial images or video recordings
  • Personal identification information
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Location information
  • Contact information (unless you voluntarily contact support)

Camera Usage

CheerLens requires camera access to analyze your facial expressions in real-time. The camera is used solely for:

  • Real-time facial expression detection
  • Calculating smile confidence scores
  • Providing practice feedback

All processing happens instantly and locally. No camera footage is recorded or stored.

Photo Library Access

If you choose to send feedback through the app, you may optionally attach a screenshot. Photo library access is only used for this purpose and is completely optional.

Subscription Information

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing securely.

No Account Required

CheerLens does not require you to create an account. All app data is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

CheerLens uses the following third-party services:

  • Google ML Kit Face Detection: Used for local facial expression analysis. All processing happens on your device.
  • Apple App Store: Used for subscription management and payment processing.

Neither of these services receives your facial data or personal information.

Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device, your data is secured by:

  • iOS device encryption
  • Local processing (no network transmission)
  • No cloud storage

Children's Privacy

CheerLens is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children, because we don't collect personal information at all.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Support section in the app or email us at michaellin5477@gmail.com.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect or store your personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. Your privacy is protected by design.
