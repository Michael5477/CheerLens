Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Introduction

CheerLens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing is done locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Store data on external servers
  • Transmit data over the internet
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services

3. Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Practice session history (stored on device only)
  • Smile detection results (stored on device only)
  • App settings and preferences (stored on device only)
  • Purchase status (managed by Apple's App Store)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

4. Camera Access

CheerLens requires camera access to analyze your facial expressions. All camera processing is done in real-time on your device. No images or video are stored, recorded, or transmitted.

5. Internet Connection

CheerLens works completely offline. No internet connection is required for core functionality. The only time an internet connection is used is for:

  • In-app purchases (processed by Apple's App Store)
  • Optional feedback submission (if you choose to send feedback)

6. In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple handles all payment processing according to their privacy policy.

7. Children's Privacy

CheerLens does not knowingly collect information from children. Since we do not collect any data, there is no risk of collecting children's information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the in-app feedback feature.

10. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:

  • You can delete the app at any time, which removes all local data
  • You can clear app data through your device settings
  • You have full control over your data since it never leaves your device
